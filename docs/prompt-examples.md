# PRISM Prompt Examples

Use these as consistent, recruiter-friendly examples.

## Career
Title: Leave full-time product design to freelance
Category: Career
Description: I want to leave my role in 2 months to freelance full time. I have 2 signed clients and 4 months of savings.
Context: I can live on low expenses, but I want to grow a design studio and avoid cash flow gaps.

## Startup
Title: Build a curated micro-SaaS studio for non-technical founders
Category: Startup
Description: Offer end-to-end MVP builds with fixed scope and 3-week timelines for solo founders.
Context: I have a small team, a 10k runway, and 3 inbound leads.

## Creative
Title: Launch a weekly creator analysis newsletter
Category: Creative
Description: Break down creator business models and revenue strategies in a weekly email.
Context: I can write 6 hours per week and already have 1,200 followers.

## Financial
Title: Sell my car to fund a part-time bootcamp
Category: Financial
Description: I want to sell my car to fund a 6-month bootcamp while working a part-time job.
Context: I live in a city with transit and can borrow a car on weekends.

## Personal
Title: Move to a new city to reset my career
Category: Personal
Description: I want to relocate within 3 months to focus on a new role and lifestyle change.
Context: I have 6 months of savings, no dependents, and remote work options.
